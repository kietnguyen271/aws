### CloudThinker – Building Agentic AI & Context Optimization with Amazon Bedrock

**- Date:** Friday, December 05, 2025  
**- Time:** 9:00 AM  
**- Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City  
**- Role:** Attendee  
**- Status:** Past Event

---

## Event Overview

This technical seminar, hosted by **CloudThinker in collaboration with AWS**, focused on building **Agentic AI systems**, optimizing context processing, and enhancing AI reasoning capabilities using **Amazon Bedrock**.  
The session provided deep insights into practical architectures, development workflows, and enterprise use cases for next-generation AI systems.

**The main topics covered in the event included:**

---

## 1. Agentic AI Architecture with Amazon Bedrock

- Introduction to Amazon Bedrock and its ecosystem.  
- How foundation models (Claude, Llama, Titan) operate within agent workflows.  
- Core components of Agentic AI: planning, reasoning, tool usage, and task execution.  
- Enterprise applications: automation, decision support, and data-driven workflows.

---

## 2. Context Optimization & Memory Layer

- Techniques for reducing and optimizing the context window.  
- Implementing “AI memory” to improve long-term interaction quality.  
- Comparison between embedding-based memory and long-context LLMs.  
- **Live Demo:** storing, retrieving, and optimizing conversational context.

---

## 3. Advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

- Designing a robust RAG architecture for enterprise applications.  
- Choosing the right vector store: OpenSearch, Aurora, DynamoDB + Vector Engine.  
- Improving retrieval precision and reducing hallucinations.  
- Strategies for handling domain-specific knowledge safely.

---

## 4. Building Multi-step Agent Workflows

- Designing agents capable of:  
  + breaking down tasks,  
  + planning sequences,  
  + making API calls,  
  + processing complex data.  
- **Hands-on Demo:** implementing multi-step workflows using **Bedrock Agents**.  
- Real-world use cases: customer support, automation pipelines, data processing agents.

---

## 5. Guardrails & Responsible AI Practices

- Implementing Bedrock Guardrails for safe and controlled content generation.  
- Detecting and blocking harmful or sensitive outputs.  
- Embedding Responsible AI principles throughout the development lifecycle.

---

## Key Takeaways & Learnings

- Agentic AI is the next major evolution of GenAI, enabling AI systems to become **autonomous, task-driven, and context-aware**.  
- Amazon Bedrock provides a secure, scalable, and enterprise-ready platform for deploying agentic workflows.  
- Context optimization and memory design greatly influence the quality and cost-efficiency of AI interactions.  
- Advanced RAG ensures that generative models can reliably use enterprise-specific knowledge.  
- Guardrails are essential for enforcing safety, compliance, and responsible AI behavior.

---

## Applications to Work

1. **Building internal ChatOps solutions** powered by Agentic AI for documentation and troubleshooting support.  
2. **Optimizing AI workflows** using memory + RAG to reduce token costs while improving reliability.  
3. **Implementing Bedrock Agents** to automate reports, data analysis, and routine operations.  
4. **Strengthening AI security** by integrating Guardrails to control outputs and ensure safe generation.  
5. **Establishing a long-term AI foundation** using Bedrock to support scalable, high-performance enterprise AI workloads.
